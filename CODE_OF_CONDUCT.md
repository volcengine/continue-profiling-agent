# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and directly relevant to the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of review or issue discussion
- publishing private information without permission

## Project Spaces

This applies to issues, pull requests, code review, and other repository discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
